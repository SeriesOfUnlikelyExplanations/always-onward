Elite Dangerous

Ships guide!

First question is which ship should I use. There are tons of options - big/small, combat/exploration, etc. I personally decided on 4 ships - an explorer, a multi-purpose, a cargo, and a combat. I have purchased all but the combat ship so far - which is less about money and more about rank.

Engineering materials:
  1. Use Inara.cv to make Crafting Lists and track Missing Components.
  2. Go to Bug Killer (HIP 16613, Planet 1 A, Coordinates -11.0093, -95.6755) for 15 minutes to cover your Raw/Encoded.
  3. Go to Dav's Hope (Hyades Sector DR-V C2-23, Planet A 5, Coordinates 44.818, -31.389) till you have all your common to rare Manufactured.
  4. Hunt for High Grade Emissions (with emmissions scanner) in high population any populated systems to cross-trade for your very rare Manufactured. Alternative is running VIP missions at Robigo system when allied can net your 4-5 g5 material per cabin you're running, on a 10 minute round trip. Start at Sothis outpost which has a manufactured trader.
